How to add a new lesson

Create a new GitHub repository (e.g. articles-indefinis-lesson), upload your index.html, then enable GitHub Pages under Settings → Pages → Branch: main. Your lesson instantly appears at thetamoul.github.io/articles-indefinis-lesson/. Finally, replace a "Coming soon" card in this hub page with a live lesson card pointing to the new URL.
